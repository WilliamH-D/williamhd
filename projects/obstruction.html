Obstruction

Started: Feb 2016 | Finished: Dec 2016

Play Store Link: Obstruction (For touch-screen Android devices, the WebGL ported Itch.io version will most likely provide a better experience)

Controls: All actions are done using the left mouse button

Instructions: Clear the way for the block as it makes its way towards the exit by removing all of the obstructions in its path.

Obstruction was a game I uploaded to the Google Play Store for Android devices when I was 15 (I published it shortly after turning 16). It was the first large project I'd worked on, and consequently, the quality isn't exactly up to the standards that I can achieve today. The version embedded into this site has been altered slightly to make it usable on desktop as a WebGL application.

This project was made in the Unity Game Engine, and programmed in the C# language.

Learning Python in my GCSE Computer Science lessons is what inspired me to make this. I greatly enjoyed the coding aspects of the subject (after all, it lead me into pursuing a Computer Science degree), and I greatly enjoy playing video games in my free time, therefore I decided that I would teach myself how to make and publish a mobile game.

There were many problems I faced due to my lack of experience in programming and game design, however I persevered through each of them and maintained the willpower to keep pushing on until eventually, I was happy with the product and was successfully able to upload it to the Google Play Store.

The music used is Mirror Mirror and Pad Music by DL Sounds (royalty free).
