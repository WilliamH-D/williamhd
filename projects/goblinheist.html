Goblin Heist

Started: Apr 2021 | Finished: Apr 2021

Ludum Dare Link: Goblin Heist

Controls: Move character - WASD/ARROW KEYS

Instructions: Plan out your movements to steal all of the goblins' treasure without being seen! But be quick if you want to get a high score!

Goblin Heist was the entry made for the 48th Ludum Dare game jam by myself and a couple of friends.

This jam's theme was "Deeper and deeper", so due to limited time, we interpreted it simply by having our thief traverse down the different levels of the goblin den.

We made this game in under 72 hours. The three of us working on it were quite busy at this time though, so we spent closer to around 48 hours on it. As we knew we would have less time, we tried to limit the scale and ambition of the project and instead focused on making the gameplay as polished and intuitive as possible. We are very proud of what we have managed to achive in such a short amount of time, and feel that this was our best entry so far.

We used the Unity Game Engine and C# to produce this game. The royalty free music used is credited in game.
