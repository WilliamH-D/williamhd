About Me

Hi, I'm Will! I am a student in my second year of studying the Computer Science Tripos at the University of Cambridge!

My main interests within Computer Science include software development, game design, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

I have long wanted to be a computer scientist and I am very excited to see how the subject will impact the future with ever progressing technology, especially in the field of AI.

I can use many programming languages including Java, C, C++, C#, Python, OCaml, SystemVerilog, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. My preferred languages are Python and Java.

I have some work experience in software development from the summer of 2018, and I hope to gain a lot more over the coming years!

When I'm not working on my course, I enjoy rowing for the Magdalene Boat Club (Yeah Magdalene!), playing casual Eton Fives, taking part in hackathons/game jams, and spending time with my friends.
